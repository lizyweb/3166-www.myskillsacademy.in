Introduction
Information Technology (IT) revolutionizes how we interact, work, and innovate in the modern world. IT encompasses the study, design, development, implementation, support, and management of computer-based information systems. From hardware to software, networks to databases, IT permeates every aspect of our daily lives, driving efficiency, connectivity, and progress. It empowers businesses to streamline operations, enables individuals to access vast resources of knowledge, and facilitates global communication and collaboration. With its ever-evolving nature, IT remains at the forefront of technological advancement, shaping the digital landscape and shaping the future of society.
