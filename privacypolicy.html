Privacy Policy

Welcome to My Skills Academy! Protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information is our top priority. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our website and services.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, and any other details provided during course registration or inquiries.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, and usage data collected via cookies to improve user experience.
  • Payment Details: Information required to process course payments securely through trusted third-party payment gateways.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide training, consultancy, and placement services.
  • Communicate updates about courses, schedules, and events.
  • Customize and enhance your learning experience.
  • Ensure website functionality and security.
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our policies.

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information. However, we may share it with:

  • Service Providers: To process payments or manage communications.
  • Partners: For institutional or corporate training collaborations.
  • Legal Authorities: If required by law or to protect our rights.

4. Data Security

We use industry-standard encryption and secure servers to protect your data. However, no online platform is 100% secure. We recommend safeguarding your login credentials and contacting us if you notice any suspicious activity.

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Cookies help us improve your browsing experience. By using our website, you consent to the use of cookies. You can adjust your browser settings to disable cookies, though some functionalities may be affected.

6. Third-Party Links

Our website may include links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices. We encourage you to review the policies of these sites before sharing personal information.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request corrections or updates to your information.
  • Opt-out of receiving promotional emails.

To exercise these rights, please contact us at rsk@myskillsacademy.in.

8. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be reflected on this page with a revised effective date.
